COMPARATOR
Compare Housing Price Value Compare Crime Rate-Temperature

Comparator is an application which lets you compare and analyze how house prices in a particular year of city or state and crime rate-temperature in a particular state vary.The application aims at enabling you to explore and visualize important data while deciding to buy/rent/sell property in the united states.There are two parts of the application - Click on either of the following TWO links to visualize and analyze data that interests you !
